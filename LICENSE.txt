DATA CONTENTS
-------------
This dataset includes:
- EPA GHGI greenhouse gas emission data (2022)
- USEEIO sector mappings and classifications
- Activity categorizations and fuel classifications
- Economic intensity calculations using USEEIO industry output data
- Enrichment metadata from EPA GHGI source tables
